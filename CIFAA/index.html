About CIFA

At CIFAA, our goal is to empower smallholder farmers in Africa through the use of collective intelligence. We connect farmers to customers looking for fresh, locally sourced produce through our peer-to-peer platform. Our platform utilizes cluster farming and cluster marketing strategies to provide farmers with the necessary resources, support, and market opportunities to succeed. By joining one of our farmer clusters, farmers gain access to training, financing, and other support services, as well as a larger customer base through our online platform.

Our mission is to support smallholder farmers in Africa and promote sustainable agriculture practices. We believe that by providing farmers with the tools and resources they need to thrive, we can improve the livelihoods of smallholder farmers and contribute to the economic development of the region. Our platform is dedicated to creating a sustainable future for smallholder farmers in Africa through collective intelligence and support.
